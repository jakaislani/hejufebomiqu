How tall is Putin's chef?

Russian oligarch Yevgeny Viktorovich Prigozhin has close links to Russian President Vladimir Putin.

In an Associated Press piece, Prigozhin was dubbed Putin’s chef because of his restaurants and catering firms, which held dinners for Putin and international dignitaries.

Prigozhin is the owner of a network of companies, three of which are suspected of meddling in the 2016 US elections.

Prigozhin is also suspected of seeking to sway the 2018 midterm elections in the United States.

In the United States, Prigozhin, his enterprises, and connections face economic sanctions as well as criminal charges.

Prigozhin was sanctioned by the US Treasury Department in December 2016 under Executive Order 13661 for giving support to senior Russian Federation officials.

The European Union sanctioned the Internet Research Agency in February 2022 for waging disinformation campaigns to manipulate public opinion and actively supporting actions that undermine and threaten Ukraine’s territorial integrity, sovereignty, and independence.

Yevgeny Prigozhin height: How tall is Putin’s Chef?

Yevgeny stands at a height of 5 feet 8 inches which is equivalent to (1.72 m) tall.

How much does Yevgeny Prigozhin weigh?

He also weighs 175 Ibs in weight (79 kg).
