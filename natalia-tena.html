Natalia Tena (born on 1 November 1984) in London, England. She is an English actress and musician. She is best known for the role of Nymphadora Tonks in the Harry Potter film series. She starred as the wildling Osha in the HBO series Game of Thrones.

Career

Tena is also the lead vocalist and accordionist of Molotov Jukebox (Musical Group). In 2014, the band released their debut album named “Carnival Flower” and “in Spring” by presenting their single “Neon Lights”.

After that, they released their second studio album, Tropical Gypsy (2016), which was released on 15 April 2016. Its lead introduced its single, “Pineapple Girl”.
