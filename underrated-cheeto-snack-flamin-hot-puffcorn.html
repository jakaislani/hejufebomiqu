This Is the Most Underrated Cheeto Product, According to Trevor Evarts

Welcome to Mythical Kitchen Picks, the series in which the culinary experts from Mythical Kitchen tell you all about their favorite foods and ingredients. This week, the team is letting you in on all their favorite cookout buys—from meats to grill to condiments to snacks. And now, it’s Trevor Evarts’ turn! You don’t have to be a cook to appreciate his pick. He skipped anything that requires heat or knife skills and went straight for a snack that will turn heads at any cookout. 

Trevor’s Pick: Chester’s Flamin’ Hot Puffcorn

Chester Cheetah has produced a myriad of delicious, cheesy corn puff snacks. Yes, I did google if Cheetos products were considered chips and it turns out they are not, but that doesn’t matter. What does matter is that they are delicious, and one product in particular does not get the praise it deserves. That product is Chester’s Flamin’ Hot Puffcorn. It’s the unsung hero of Chester Cheetah’s arsenal of mouth watering snack foods. If you’ve ever had Pirate’s Booty, imagine that puffy airy texture, flavored like a flamin’ hot Cheeto. Bring a bag of Chester’s Flamin’ Hot Puffcorn to your family cookout and you will be raised on a dais of snack royalty, taking your rightful place as the monarch of all snackdom.
