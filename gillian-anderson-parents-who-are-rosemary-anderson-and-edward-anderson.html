Gillian Anderson Parents: Who are Rosemary Anderson and Edward Anderson?

Rosemary Anderson and Edward Anderson are the parents of famous actress, Gillian Anderson.

Rosemary Anderson profession

What is Rosemary Anderson’s profession? What does Rosemary Anderson do for work? Rosemary Anderson is a computer analyst who later became the vice president of Neurofibromatosis Inc., the neurofibromatosis support group of West Michigan.

Edward Anderson profession

What was Edward Anderson’s profession? What did Edward Anderson do for work? Edward Anderson owned a film post-production company.

Edward Anderson education

What was Edward Anderson’s educational background? Which school did Edward Anderson attend? Where did Edward Anderson attend school? Edward Anderson attended the London Film School.

There is very little information about them but it is known that they had two other children; Zoe Anderson and Aaron Anderson. Unfortunately, Aaron died in September 2011 after a prolonged battle with brain cancer.

The family lived in London for quite a long time in the 1980s due to Edward Anderson attending film school.
