Frankie Grande husband: Who is Hale Leon?

Frank James Michael Grande Marchione is an American dancer, actor, singer, producer, television host and YouTuber. Grande is the older half-brother of singer and actress Ariana Grande.

Frankie Grande’s husband: Who is Hale Leon?

The 39-year-old stage and screen performer married his fiancé Hale Leon at the Grande family home in Boca Raton, Florida, in a small, Star Wars-themed ceremony on May 4.

Frankie’s Instagram post concluded with a heartfelt note about his passionate love for Leon, 29. “I can’t wait to share all of these amazing stories, pics, and videos with you all as slowly as we get them, but for now, let me just say I am the happiest I’ve ever been,” wrote the Henry Danger star. “I’m married to the man of my dreams, our families have merged so beautifully, and we are well on our way to happily ever after. I love you Hale Grande. I am so honored to be your husband.”
