About

Professional inside linebacker that was selected by the Green Bay Packers as a first round pick in the 2022 NFL Draft. Prior to being drafted, he played college football at the University of Georgia. While at Georgia, he won the 2021 CFP National Championship.

He was once considered a four-star prospect during his time at Crisp County High School. He also won a Regional title as a member of his school's varsity basketball team in 2017.

He made his official NFL debut while playing against the Minnesota Vikings during week one of the 2022 NFL regular season. He played in all 18 regular season games as a rookie.

He was born and raised in Cordele, Georgia.

He was college teammates with Stetson Bennett at the University of Georgia.
