Official: MAD locks its OTT premiere date

Official: MAD locks its OTT premiere date

Published on Oct 30, 2023 8:02 pm IST

Earlier today, we reported that the Telugu super hit movie MAD, recently released, is likely to make its digital debut on Netflix on November 3, 2023.

As anticipated, Netflix has now officially confirmed the news. The streaming platform has just announced that the movie will be exclusively available for streaming in Telugu starting this Friday.

In this film directed by Kalyan Shankar, Narne Nithin, Sangeeth Shobhan, Ram Nithin, Sri Gouri Priya Reddy, Ananathika Sanilkumar, and Gopikaa Udyan, played significant roles.

MAD was jointly produced by Haarika Suryadevara and Sai Sowjanya under the banners of Haarika & Hassine Creations and Fortune Four Cinemas. Producer Naga Vamsi presented the movie, and the music was composed by director Bheems Ceciroleo. Stay connected with us for more OTT updates.
