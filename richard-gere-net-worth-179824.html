Richard Gere Net Worth

Richard Gere is an American actor born on August 31, 1949 in Philadelphia, PA. He is best known for his leading roles in the films Pretty Woman, Chicago, Runaway Bride, Primal Fear, First Knight, and The Jackal. Gere has been a major presence in Hollywood for decades and continues to be a highly sought-after actor.

About

Leading man who starred in Pretty Woman and Chicago. His other major films include Runaway Bride, Primal Fear, First Knight, and The Jackal.

Before Fame

He earned a scholarship to the University of Massachusetts for gymnastics. He earned a supporting role in Looking for Mr. Goodbar in the 1970s.

Trivia

He became a sex symbol as a result of his role in American Gigolo in the 1980s.

Family Life

He married model Cindy Crawford in 1991, but the couple separated in 1995. He then married Carey Lowell in 2002. He and Carey separated in 2013, then he briefly dated Padma Lakshmi in 2014. He has a son named Homer.

Associated With

He was in Pretty Woman with Julia Roberts.
