Bryce Dallas Howard Net Worth

Bryce Dallas Howard is an American actress born on March 2, 1981 in Los Angeles, CA. She is the daughter of Ron Howard and made her debut in the 2004 M. Night Shyamalan feature The Village. She has since gone on to receive a Golden Globe Award nomination for her performance in the TV movie As You Like It, and has starred in the blockbuster Jurassic World and the Disney film Pete's Dragon.

About

Actress and daughter of Ron Howard who landed her first major film role in the 2004 M. Night Shyamalan feature The Village. She received a Golden Globe Award nomination for her performance in the TV movie As You Like It. She also starred in the 2015 blockbuster Jurassic World and the 2016 Disney film Pete's Dragon.

Before Fame

She attended numerous arts schools, including the Tisch School at New York University.

Trivia

She played Gwen Stacey in the 2007 film Spider-Man 3.

Family Life

She married Seth Gabel in June of 2006. The couple welcomed a son named Theodore in 2007 and a daughter named Beatrice in 2012.

Associated With

She played a prominent role opposite Emma Stone in the critically acclaimed 2011 film The Help.
