T-Bar Calls Out AEW Virgin For Stealing His Finisher, Sammy Guevara Responds To T-Bag

We officially have some wrestling Twitter drama to kick off 2021 the right way.

It all started when Retribution’s T-Bar (Dominik Dijakovic) said that a virgin on All Elite Wrestling stole his finisher after working with him on the indies.

He then said that he’d steal a move from Guevara to get back at him, but all he has are moves that Ricochet does.

Some little teenage virgin on AEW stole my finisher like four years ago after we did a show together. I’d steal something from his move set but it’s all just @KingRicochet moves.

— T-BAR (@TBARRetribution) January 13, 2021

Guevara then struck back by calling him “T-Bag,” and saying that the one that originated the move was Mott Demorest. Guevara says he’s “just trying to get the move to be seen since [T-Bag is] never on TV.”

Someone tell T-Bag the move actually belongs to Matt Demorest (the guy he stole it from) & I’m just trying to get the move to be seen since you know he’s never on TV.

Also while you are sitting doing nothing in catering on Monday check out the newest vlog https://t.co/WvS8VDfZRu

— sammy guevara (@sammyguevara) January 13, 2021

The burns kept coming from FTR’s Dax Harwood, who called T-Bar an idiot that is lucky to be in the business.

“Stole MY finisher”. MY FINISHER?! ? The guy is an idiot. He’s lucky to even be in the business.

— Uncle Dax FTR (@DaxFTR) January 13, 2021

Let’s look forward to plenty of additional beef in the new year.
