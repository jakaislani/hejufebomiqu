Steven Douglas Agee is an American comedian, actor, musician, writer, and director. Agee is best known for his roles as Steve Myron on The Sarah Silverman Program and King Shark in the Suicide Squad soft sequel/reboot.

Agee gained fame by posting controversial or comedy videos online before becoming a star. This included clips called “Black Mammie” and “Happy Halloween” as well as other holiday-themed clips.

Career

Steve Agee started a podcast called Steve Agee. His previous job was being a cast member on the Dungeons and Dragons tabletop roleplaying game podcast, Nerd Poker, but he left when the show temporarily went on break (he is now an occasional guest). He co-hosts a podcast with Busy Philipps called We’re No Doctors, in which the pair discuss medical issues.

In addition to acting and comedy, Agee is also a musician. Throughout his teenage years, he played guitar and bass in various heavy metal bands, and he has worked with Brendon Small in the past.

In 2021, Steve Agee released a comedy-punk album, Scab, after playing bass guitar in a Rocksmith commercial in 2014.

Additionally, Agee has written 337 episodes of the show named ‘Jimmy Kimmel Live’ which was started in 2005 and ended in 2008. Also, he has written The Andy Milonakis Show.
