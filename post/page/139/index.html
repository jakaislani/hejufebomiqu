There also are several long-running gags, the unfunniest being about a hit man who is turned into the hapless victim of accidents and near-fatal emergency treatments. They’re not the dull, milquetoast, suburban neighbors of potato chips that people often make them out to be. Classic Lay’s are actually full of flavor, and that flavor, friends, is fat.
